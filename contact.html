Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update my business information in the directory?

To update your business information, please contact our support team with your business ID and the details you'd like to update. You can reach us at help@citytel.com or call +994 55 334 32 22.

Is there a mobile app available for the city telephone services?

Yes, we have mobile apps available for both iOS and Android devices. You can download them from the respective app stores by searching for "CityTel".

How often is the information in the directory updated?

We update our directory information on a weekly basis. However, critical updates such as emergency service numbers are updated immediately as they change.

Can I request to have my personal information removed from the directory?

Yes, you can request to have your personal information removed from our directory. Please submit a removal request through our contact form or call our support line.
