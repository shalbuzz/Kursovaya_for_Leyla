About Us

Learn more about our mission and the team behind City Telephone Services

Our Story

Established in 2010, City Telephone Services was created to address the growing need for a centralized, accessible information system for city residents and visitors.

What started as a simple directory has evolved into a comprehensive platform that connects citizens with essential services, government offices, and local businesses.

Our mission is to provide accurate, up-to-date information that helps strengthen community connections and improve access to city resources.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide a reliable, user-friendly information system that connects citizens with the services they need, when they need them.

Vision

To be the most trusted resource for city information, fostering a more connected and informed community.

Values

Accuracy, accessibility, reliability, and community service are at the heart of everything we do.
